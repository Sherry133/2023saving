Getting Started to SAVE AT SCHOOL is Easy!

School Savings is a processor for the Federal Reserve Bank.

It's a process...


School Savings is a cooperative venture between a local bank and it's community schools. The bank provides the free saving account and student incentives while the school's PTA/PTO group manages the school banking process. School Savings provides the deposit software and electroncially credits the students' deposits to their saving account through the Federal Reserve Bank.

For Schools

Call 888-787-7728 or email support@schoolsavings.com to receive assistance in finding a local bank sponsor.

For Banks and Credit Unions

Select a local school and send Account Enrollment Packets home with students.

Ask PTA/PTO to select Volunteers to collect student deposits and deliver them to participating bank branch.

Advertise First Deposit Day CONTEST to students, parents and media to FIRE-UP participation.

Receive School Savings login credentials. 888-787-7728 | CONTACT: support@schoolsavings.com

For '529 College Savings' Sponsors

Select a local school and send 529 Account Enrollment Packets home with students.

Notify parents to register students at www.schoolsavings.com. Organize PTA/PTO Volunteers to accept deposits.

Create and advertise a First Deposit Day CONTEST to students, parents and the media to increase participation.

Initiate electronic deposit processing with School Savings. CONTACT: 888-787-7728 | support@schoolsavings.com
